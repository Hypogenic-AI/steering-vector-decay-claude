# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers on Steering Vector Decay

1. **[Steering Language Models With Activation Engineering](2308.10248_activation_engineering.pdf)**
   - Authors: Turner, Thiergart, Leech, Udell, Vazquez, Mini, MacDiarmid (2023)
   - arXiv: 2308.10248 | Citations: 401
   - Why relevant: Foundational ActAdd method for activation steering

2. **[Steering Llama 2 via Contrastive Activation Addition](2312.06681_contrastive_activation_addition.pdf)**
   - Authors: Rimsky, Gabrieli, Schulz, Tong, Hubinger, Turner (2024)
   - arXiv: 2312.06681 | Citations: 579
   - Why relevant: Standard CAA method, behavioral question datasets

3. **[Multi-property Steering with Dynamic Activation Composition](2406.17563_dynamic_activation_composition.pdf)**
   - Authors: Scalena, Sarti, Nissim (2024)
   - arXiv: 2406.17563 | Citations: 26
   - Why relevant: **Directly compares Start/Fixed/Dim/Dynamic steering schedules; shows first-step-only steering fails**

4. **[Comparative Analysis of SAE and Activation Difference](2510.01246_sparse_autoencoder_comparison.pdf)**
   - Authors: Xie (2025)
   - arXiv: 2510.01246
   - Why relevant: **Proposes token-wise decaying steering strategy with formal decay formula**

5. **[Steering Language Models in Multi-Token Generation](2509.12065_steering_multi_token.pdf)**
   - Authors: Klerings, Brinkmann, Ruffinelli, Ponzetto (2025)
   - arXiv: 2509.12065
   - Why relevant: **Studies steering strength, location, and duration effects in multi-token generation**

6. **[Inference-Time Intervention](2306.03341_inference_time_intervention.pdf)**
   - Authors: Li, Patel, Viégas, Pfister, Wattenberg (2023)
   - arXiv: 2306.03341 | Citations: 951
   - Why relevant: ITI truthfulness steering method, uses continuous intervention

7. **[KV Cache Steering for Controlling Frozen LLMs](2507.08799_kv_cache_steering.pdf)**
   - Authors: Belitsky, Kopiczko, Dorkenwald, Mirza, Glass, Snoek, Asano (2025)
